Chicken Pathia

Description

A pathia is a moderately hot curry that embodies the battle between sweet and sour to bring you a crowd pleasing dish.

Ingredients

    1kg -Chicken thighs, boneless skinless thighs or breast. 400g - Onions 60g - Red pepper 3 Tbsp - Garlic & ginger paste 2 Tbsp - Mild madras curry powder 1 Tbsp - Garam masala 1 Tsp - Turmeric 1/2 Tbsp - Tandoori masala powder 1 - star anise 1 Tsp - Salt (to taste) 1 Thumb size piece - Cassia bark or cinnamon stick 2 1/2 Tbsp - Mango chutney 1 Tbsp - Tamarind sauce 6 Tbsp - Tomato puree 2 Tsp - Hot chilli powder 2 Tsp - Red kashmiri chilli powder 1 Tsp - Kasoori methi 500ml - Boiling water (Coriander to garnish)
