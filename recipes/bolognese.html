Tagliatelle Bolognese

Description

Another simple but heavenly recipe. Paired with the "meaty" tagliatelle, this is a real comfort food style dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 - Whole Carrot
  • 4 Tbsp - Olive oil
  • 1/2 - Large onion (Finely diced)
  • 800g - Lean minced beef
  • 50ml - Red wine
  • 50ml - Milk
  • 500ml - Passata
  • 1 - Beef stock cube
  • 300ml - Boiling water
  • 4 Tbsp - Tomato puree

Method
